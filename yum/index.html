lemon surprise pudding

serves 6

cooking time 30 minutes to 1 hour

  • 17 fluid ounces milk
  • 3 1/2 ounces unsalted butter, softened
  • 6 ounces plain coaster sugar
  • 3 lemons
  • 3 ounces plain flour
  • 2 medium free-range eggs
3 1/2 pint shallow oven proof dish
    1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
    2 Butter a 3 1/2 pint shallow ovenproof dish.
    3 Grate the zest from two of the lemons, then squeeze juice out of all three
    4 Cream the butter with the sugar and lemon zest.
    5 Beat in the egg yolks one by one. do not panic if the mixture curdles.
    6 Beat in the flour, a spoonful alternating with milk and lemon juice.
    7 Whisk the egg whites until they form stiff peak.
    8 Then fold lightly into the lemon batter.
    9 Pour in the mixture into the baking dish, stand the dish in a roasting tin.
    10 Pour enough boiling water around the dish to about halfway up the sides.
    11 Bake in the oven for 40-50 minutes until the pudding is golden brown.
    12 Serve hot or warm, with or without cream
